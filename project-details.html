Into the media era, Hong Kong Design Centre will focus far more on two-way communications than ever before, participating together with people from all walks of life, to explain the value of design and innovation.

Hong Kong Design Centre is like an exchange platform, and diverse design information, knowledge and activities will be brought to the design industry, businesses and the public

Because of the multi-directional exchange of information, the Hong Kong Design Centre has become more valuable, and the different plans have become a platform for people from all walks of life to participate in.

Enabling designers, design events, design works and design information to become the focus for all sectors.

Graphic System

Brand Approach

The visual identity of Hong Kong Design Centre aims to bring out its unique role as a forerunner in design and a catalyst for the growth of the creative industries.

Spotlight is the concept for the brand’s visual identity, which implies focusing on the industries’ ideas, creativity, and technology. Creating spotlights for every creative talent to shine so that designers, design events, design creations and design information can become the focus of all industries’ attention as if they were in the spotlight on the stage.
